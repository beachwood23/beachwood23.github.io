FeedMePls Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 21, 2026

FeedMePls does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. This policy explains what that means in practice.

Data collection

None. FeedMePls collects no personal information, usage data, analytics, or crash reports.

Data storage

All feeding entries you log are stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device. It is not backed up to any server operated by us, and it is not accessible to anyone other than you.

If you use iCloud Backup, your device backup may include app data as part of Apple's standard iOS backup — this is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy, not ours.

Third parties

FeedMePls does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, analytics services, or tracking tools. No data is shared with any third party.

Children

FeedMePls is intended for use by parents and caregivers. It does not collect any data from any user, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions about privacy? apps@beachwood23.com
