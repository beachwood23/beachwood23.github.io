About

We recently had a baby, and my wife wanted to track her breast and bottle feeding times.

When we were looking at the available apps , every single one had some subscription payments, wanted your email address and contact information, or both. All we wanted was a simple tracker that wasn't going to track us back.

The goal for FeedMePls is the simplest possible tool that covered our use cases: knowing when the last feed was, how long it went, and whether there's a pattern worth noticing. There is no subscription, no account required, and no data collected. You track your data, and the app doesn't track you!

Requires iOS 17 or later.

Privacy

All data lives on your device. FeedMePls collects nothing. There are no accounts, no servers, no analytics, no cloud sync. Everything you log stays in local storage on your iPhone and never leaves it. You can delete the app and everything goes with it.
